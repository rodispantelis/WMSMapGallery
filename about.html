Privacy statement

WMS Map Viewer uses cookies to collect anonymous data for statistical reasons and advertisement display. Collected data for statistical reasons are not connected in anyway to user identities or to any third-party service; they are anonymous and they regard the frequence of use of the service.
If you care to stop anonymous data collection you may disable cookies in your browser or enable tracking protection. Moreover, in Firefox you may activate private browsing and in Chrome open the app in incognito windows. The functionality of the application will not be affected by any of these actions; only anonymous data collection and advertisement display will be affected.
